Old Fashioned

A timeless whiskey cocktail

The Old Fashioned is one of the oldest known cocktails, dating back to the early 1800s. This classic drink showcases the flavors of quality bourbon or rye whiskey, complemented by subtle sweetness and aromatic bitters.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 1 sugar cube (or 1/4 oz simple syrup)
  • 2-3 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange peel for garnish

Steps

  1. Place sugar cube in an Old Fashioned glass
  2. Add bitters and a small splash of water
  3. Muddle until sugar is dissolved
  4. Add whiskey and stir
  5. Add one large ice cube or several smaller ones
  6. Garnish with an orange peel
